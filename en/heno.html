Oat Hay

Top-quality forage, ideal as a main feed or supplement for livestock. Oat hay provides high fiber content and good palatability, making it a highly valued option among farmers and ranchers.

Overview of semi-chopped bale

Each bale weighs approximately 500–550 kg, with dimensions of 220–230 x 80 x 70 cm. Its firm pressing allows efficient and stable stacking. The greenish-golden color and fresh aroma indicate excellent preservation and nutritional value.

We maintain constant stock year-round. We offer fast deliveries with products always ready for immediate use.

Oat hay is highly digestible and low in moisture. Ideal for ruminants, horses, and other animals requiring high-quality fiber. Its sustainable and controlled production ensures trust and reliability with every delivery.
