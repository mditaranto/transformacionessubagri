Pressed wheat in small bales

Ideal format for small farms, pets, horticulture, or household use. Straw pressed into compact packages, easy to handle, store, and transport.

Compact and lightweight package

The weight of each bale ranges from 20 to 23 kg. Approximate dimensions: 42 x 50 x 34 cm. Perfect for those who need small quantities of straw without machinery or special logistics.

Front of the bale: clean and uniform cut Side of the bale: firm compression

Thanks to its reduced size, a large number of bales can be easily stacked in a small space. Ideal for retail sale or individual use. The product remains dry, clean, and dust-free.

Our small bale straw maintains the same quality guarantees: seed-free, moisture-free, and with a homogeneous texture. Perfect for horses, rabbits, chickens, and small animals.
