Pressed wheat straw
Semi-chopped fiber

Ideal product for livestock bedding or animal feed, made from selected wheat straw with semi-chopped fiber. High-density pressed for easy handling, storage, and transport. Excellent absorption and cleanliness.

Overview of semi-chopped bale

Each bale weighs approximately 600-650 kg and measures around 220-230 x 100 x 80 cm. The uniform pressing allows for easy stacking in storage. Ideal for livestock farms looking for dry and durable bedding.

Front view: compact and stable pressing Side view of the pressed bale

Sterile storage facility

We have a permanent stock at our facilities. Fast delivery and product ready for immediate use.

The straw stands out for its purity, uniform golden color, and absence of visible impurities. Its semi-chopped texture ensures even distribution and superior absorption. Each batch is carefully inspected to ensure a clean, dry, and low-dust product—ideal for enhancing animal welfare and supporting controlled feeding. Committed to always providing the highest quality.
