Compressed Pea Straw

High forage value product with excellent absorbency. Derived from pea crops, its high-density compression ensures easy handling, storage, and transport. An ideal alternative for livestock bedding and dietary supplementation.

Each bale weighs approximately 450–480 kg and measures 220–230 x 80 x 70 cm. Its compact and uniform structure allows safe stacking in barns or warehouses. The pale green color and texture make it particularly useful in agricultural and livestock operations.

We maintain constant stock throughout the year. We offer fast delivery with product always ready for immediate use.

Compressed pea straw stands out for its high fiber content and residual nutrients. It is highly valued for its versatility, low dust, and low moisture content. A natural, effective, and sustainable solution to improve livestock health and optimize producer resources.
